Yurts

What is a Yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully furnished with a queen-size bed, a private bathroom, a kitchenette, and a gas fireplace. Relax on your private deck and enjoy the view of the Pacific Ocean.
