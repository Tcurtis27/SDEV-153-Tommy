Local Clubs

Joining a local pickleball club is a great way to meet other players, improve your skills, and participate in organized play.

Benefits of club membership often include:

  • Regular play times
  • Skills clinics and lessons
  • Tournaments and ladder play
  • Social events

Tournaments

Tournaments are available for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced players. They're a fun way to test your skills and meet other players.

Tournament formats include:

  • Round robin
  • Double elimination
  • Age-based divisions
  • Skill-level divisions

Become an Ambassador

USA Pickleball Ambassadors promote the sport in their local communities. Ambassadors help introduce new players to the game and support local pickleball growth.

Volunteer Opportunities
